Profile

My name is Dirk Engels and I am a passionate software engineer living in Amsterdam. I love building systems while aiming for simplicity. In my free time I like to spend time with my family and friends, play a game of pinball, start yet another hobby software project or work on my balcony garden.

I love building technical solutions, but also enjoy bringing people together and building connections. At university I have learned how to design systems and gather requirements effectively. During my previous work experiences I have shown these skills in practice. I like to communicate with the end-users of the systems directly to get a good understanding of the issues they are facing.
